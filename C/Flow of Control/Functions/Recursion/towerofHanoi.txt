Tower of Hanoi — The Idea
You have n discs on peg S (source). Goal: move all to peg D (destination) using H (helper).
The rule: never place a bigger disc on a smaller one.

The trick: to move n discs from S→D, you —
  ① Move top (n-1) discs out of the way: S→H (using D as helper)
  ② Move the big bottom disc: S→D (just a printf)
  ③ Move the (n-1) discs from helper back on top: H→D (using S as helper)
